We work in the areas of performance marketing and influencer marketing. What is this?

Performance marketing is marketing aimed at results, expressed as an increase in leads and sales. Our team not only provides leads for the client’s products but also provides in-house developed conversion creatives. Our media buyers have already mastered 70+ traffic sources and don’t plan to stop.
Influencer marketing is marketing aimed at selling through recommendations from bloggers and Influencers in various media and social networks. We work with Youtube, Instagram, Twitch and many others.It’s a fairly popular and well-run way to increase the visibility of your product or service and get leads. People trust the recommendations of Influencers and willingly agree to buy the products they advertise.You should appreciate all the possibilities of these models!
